# 7-Day Training Plan - Batch 1

Practice goal: turn Batch 1 expressions into quick spoken responses.

## Daily Routine

Spend about 15 minutes per day:

1. Read the core sentence 3 times.
2. Replace the topic with your own work context 3 times.
3. Do 5 Chinese-to-English quick response drills.

## Day 1: Follow Up + Blockers

Core sentences:

> Just wanted to follow up on this. Have you had a chance to take a look?

> Let me know if there are any questions or blockers from your side.

Replacement drills:

- Just wanted to follow up on the legal review. Have you had a chance to take a look?
- Just wanted to follow up on the GitHub issue. Have you had a chance to check it?
- Just wanted to follow up on the data pipeline. Have you had a chance to confirm it?
- Let me know if there are any concerns from your side.
- Let me know if there's anything I can help with.
- Let me know if anything is blocking this on your side.

## Day 2: Clarify + Thank for Clarification

Core sentences:

> Just to clarify, this is not A, but B.

> Got it, thanks for clarifying.

## Day 3: Request Support + Ask Next Steps

Core sentences:

> Would you be able to help review this when you have a chance?

> Could you advise on the next steps?

## Day 4: Completed + Proposal

Core sentences:

> We've already finished this on our side.

> We have a proposal that might help.

## Day 5: Alignment + Move Forward

Core sentences:

> Yes, that makes sense to me.

> Hopefully we can move this forward soon.

## Day 6: Full Review

Say one sentence for each of the 10 Batch 1 scenarios without looking at the notes.

## Day 7: Real Work Simulation

Simulate one realistic work conversation using at least five chunks:

- Just wanted to follow up on...
- Have you had a chance to...?
- Let me know if there are any...
- Would you be able to help...?
- Could you advise on the next steps?
