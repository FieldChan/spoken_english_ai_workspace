## Self-Correction

Chinglish / weak version:

> What I mean is that if we can accept anonymization if legal requires.

Better version:

> What I mean is that we can accept anonymization if Legal requires it.

Why it is better:

- Avoid repeating "if" twice in the same clause.
- Use "requires it" because "require" needs an object here.
- Capitalize "Legal" when referring to the Legal team.
- Use "field" more often than "column" in product/legal discussions unless the database schema itself is the focus.
- "Does not affect production" is more natural than "does not affect the production."
