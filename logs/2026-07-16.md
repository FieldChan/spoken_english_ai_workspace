We recommend validating the change in the test environment before proceeding. Could you confirm whether we should create a support issue? In addition, could you advise who the appropriate contact would be for this dataset issue?

## Self-Correction

Key improvements:

- Use `complete the reconciliation` or `for reconciliation`, not `keep the data aligned`.
- Use `Could you help check whether...?`, not `Could you help if...?`.
- Use `the test environment` for a specific environment.
- Use `Also` or `In addition` to add a related question; `On the other hand` signals contrast and does not fit here.
- Keep punctuation inside sentences consistent; do not join full requests with commas.
