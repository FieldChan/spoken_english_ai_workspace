## 60-Second Simulation

Original:

> I updated PR yesterday with Frankfurt and London alignment changes. Could you please help review it when you get a chance? If where are no blockers or concerns, can you confirm whether we can move forward with deployment? Please tell me what to do next.

Better spoken version:

> I updated the PR yesterday with the Frankfurt and London alignment changes. Could you please review it when you get a chance? If there are no blockers or concerns, could you confirm whether we can move forward with deployment? Could you please advise on the next steps?

More conversational version:

> I updated the PR yesterday with the alignment changes for Frankfurt and London. Could you take a look when you get a chance? If everything looks good, can we move forward with deployment? Let me know what the next steps are.

More formal version:

> I updated the PR yesterday to include the Frankfurt and London alignment changes. Could you please review it when you have time? If there are no blockers or concerns, could you please confirm whether we may proceed with deployment? We would appreciate your guidance on the next steps.

## Self-Correction

Key improvements:

- Add `the` before `PR`, and say `the Frankfurt and London alignment changes`.
- Use `If there are no blockers or concerns`, not `If where are...`.
- Use `review it`, not `help review it`, when the direct request is already polite.
- Use `Could you please advise on the next steps?` rather than `Please tell me what to do next.`
