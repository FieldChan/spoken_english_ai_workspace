## Self-Correction

Chinglish / weak version:

> Could you please help to review the code?

Better version:

> Could you please help review the code?

Why it is better:

- Use "help review" instead of "help to review" in this request pattern.
- Split long comma-connected messages into short workplace sentences.
- Use "deployment" without "the" when talking about the process in general.
- Use plural "blockers or concerns" after "any."
