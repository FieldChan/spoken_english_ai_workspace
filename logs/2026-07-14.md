## Self-Correction

Key improvements:

- Use `Just to clarify`, not `Just want to clarify`.
- Say `If legal requires it`, with an object after `requires`.
- Use `consider anonymizing it`, not bare `consider anonymization` when referring to a specific column.
- Use `suggest validating`, not `suggest validate`.
- Use short sentences for clarification and a separate request for confirmation.
